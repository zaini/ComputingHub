Why are computer systems important?

Computer systems are important because they are used everywhere. Here are some examples:

  • Communication: computer communication like email and SMS have enabled us to contact people faster, and further away.
  • Automation: Computer systems like robots are very efficient at carrying out their jobs. Automated machines lead to a higher output in manufacturing.
  • Accuracy: Computers are much more precise, whether in manufacturing or when completing calculations. They have lead to products of higher quality and accurate information.
  • Business and finance: Online shopping has lead to a wider variety of choice and cheaper goods. Computer systems have lead to more ways of doing business, increased profits and more sales.
  • Decisions: With lots of information that can be processed faster, decisions that computers make are better informed and more accurate.
  • Safety: Computers manage aircrafts, control trains and monitor patients' bodies
  • Travel: Travel has become much easier through the translation provided by computer systems along with easier flight and hotel bookings.
